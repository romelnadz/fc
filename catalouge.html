Tips for a proper tile installation

Upload: June 18, 2018
Author: Rica Palcon   Views: 682

In order to properly lay tiles, there are various steps needed to be followed. Applying tiles is something that most people can do given a proper knowledge of doing so. Here are some basic tips to know for laying tiles properly

Prepare the surface before applying tiles, make sure that every surface is clean, smooth, dry, wax- free, soap scum-free, and grease-free.

If there are areas to repair, patch, or if there are damaged or uneven surfaces, attend to it first. Then, remove any moldings, trim, or appliances that are placed in the surface you want to lay your tiles on.
